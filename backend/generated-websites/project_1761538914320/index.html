About Us

Trattoria Bellissima is a family-owned Italian restaurant that has been serving authentic and delicious cuisine for over 20 years. Our head chef, Gianfranco Bellissimo, was born and raised in Italy and brings the flavors of his homeland to every dish we serve.

We use only the finest and freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to create a truly unforgettable dining experience. From our handmade pasta to our perfectly crafted pizzas, every bite is a testament to our commitment to quality and tradition.

Visit Us

We are located in the heart of the city, at 123 Main Street. We are open seven days a week, from 11am to 10pm.
